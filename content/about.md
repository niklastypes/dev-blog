## Hi, I'm Niklas!

an ML / AI Engineer based in Munich with a passion for building intelligent systems end-to-end and at scale.

Currently, I'm part of the AI Engineering team at one of the biggest streaming & entertainment players in the German-speaking region of Europe, developing and scaling a langgraph-based RAG agent for content discovery serving millions of users.

## What I do

I focus on applied machine learning, generative AI, and system design, turning experimental ideas into robust, production-ready solutions.

Over the past years, I’ve:
- engineered retrieval pipelines, evaluation frameworks, and prompt experimentation tools for reproducible LLM workflows
- led the technical direction for a company-wide GenAI platform, empowering thousands of non-technical users to easily generate text, images and more
- designed testing suites and CI/CD infrastructure to ensure seamless deployment and reliability at scale

## What fascinates me

Tech, computers and robots have fascinated me since my childhood.
I am therefore ecstatic to be living through the current era and witnessing how humanity starts developing towards my wildest Sci-Fi dreams - with humanoid (consumer) robots and ever-improving LLMs that make AI just feel natural and magical.
